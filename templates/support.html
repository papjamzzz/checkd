// frequently asked questions
My product was rejected. Can I appeal? ⌄
Yes — but the fastest path is to fix whatever the AI flagged and resubmit with a new or updated URL. The rejection reason is shown on the verdict screen. If you believe it was a genuine mistake, reach out on X @creativekonsole.
Why was my URL flagged as unreachable? ⌄
We ping your URL before validation. If your site was down, returning a 4xx/5xx error, or blocking bots at the time of submission, it gets flagged. Wait until your site is stable and resubmit. Railway cold starts, Vercel preview URLs, and localhost URLs will all fail this check.
Can I submit more than one product? ⌄
Yes — you can submit as many different products as you want. The one-per-product rule means each URL and domain gets one shot, not one per person. Build 10 real products, list all 10.
Is Check'd really free forever? ⌄
Yes. There is no paid placement, no featured spots for purchase, no fast-track fee. The only gate is the AI validation — and that's there to protect the quality of the directory, not to extract money from builders.
How do views and uses get counted? ⌄
Views are page loads of the products listing where your card is visible. Uses are counted when someone clicks the "Use It →" button on your card — that's an outbound click to your product URL. Both metrics are real signals, no inflation.
Can I update my listing after it's approved? ⌄
Not yet — but this is coming. For now, reach out on X @creativekonsole with your product name and what needs to change.
Who runs Check'd? ⌄
Check'd is built and run by Creative Konsoles — an indie software studio building tools for makers, traders, and creators. We built Check'd because we were frustrated with every other directory.
